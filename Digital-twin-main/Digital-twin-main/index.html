Digital Twin System for Personalized mRNA Vaccine Compatibility Assessment

The Digital Twin System for Personalized mRNA Vaccine Compatibility Assessment is an AI-based platform that predicts how safely an mRNA-LNP vaccine formulation may work for an individual before real-world administration. By creating a virtual model of a patient using basic clinical data such as age and optional genetic information, the system simulates immune response, metabolic behavior, and potential allergic reactions. It provides percentage-based predictions for side effects and overall safety, helping shift healthcare toward predictive and preventive medicine.
